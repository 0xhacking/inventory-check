Add/Remove Services

Select the Hybrid Services you want to turn on for your organization. This step lets you configure the selected Services on your on-premises devices and turn on related functionality for your users.

You are about to remove the following Hybrid Services for your organization:

If you click Confirm, for each service you remove:

  • All of your users will have the service deactivated.
  • Your Cisco Expressway will uninstall the service connector.
  • Data for the service connector will be deleted.

If your Cisco Expressway has no other services installed, you will need to deregister it. Log in and deregister from Applications > Hybrid Services > Connector Management.
