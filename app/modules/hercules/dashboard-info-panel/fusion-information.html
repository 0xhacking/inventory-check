About Cloud Extensions

Cisco Cloud Extensions empower cloud-based and premises-based solutions to deliver a more capable, better integrated collaboration user experience.

To use Cloud Extensions, register your Cisco VCS with the Collaboration Cloud to install your Connectors.

Then, you can monitor and manage Connectors from the Cisco Spark Management Portal or from your Cisco VCS.
