Connect each expressway to the cloud

While keeping this page open in your browser:

  1. In a separate tab, log on to your Expressway, and go to Applications > Spark Cloud > Cloud Extentions Service
  2. Click connect with Spark Platform
  3. Configure the service specific connectors from your expressway
  4. Return to this tab to verify the status throughout the process
  5. Repeat steps 1-4 for each cluster you want to connect with Spark. See Documentation for more details.
