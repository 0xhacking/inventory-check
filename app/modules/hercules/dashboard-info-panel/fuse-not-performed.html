Connect Your Expressways to the Cisco Collaboration Cloud

Complete the following steps, while keeping this page open in your browser:

  1. In a separate tab, log on to your Expressway, and go to Applications > Cloud Extensions > Connectors
  2. Click Register
  3. Configure the service specific connectors from your Expressway
  4. Throughout the process, return to this tab to verify the status
  5. Repeat steps 1-4 for each cluster you want to connect with Cisco Collaboration Cloud. See documentation for more details.
