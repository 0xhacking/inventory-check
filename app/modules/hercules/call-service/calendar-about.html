About Calendar Hybrid Service

Cisco Hybrid Services empower cloud-based and premises-based solutions to deliver a more capable, better integrated collaboration user experience.

To use Hybrid Services, register your Expressway with the Collaboration Cloud to install your Connectors.

Then, you can monitor and manage Connectors from the Cloud Collaboration Management Portal or from your Expressways.
