About Call Service (Hybrid Service)

Call Service (Hybrid Service) enables your users to collaborate instantly by effortlessly sharing their desktops from Cisco Spark clients while they are on their phones (Aware feature) and answer their work calls or call colleagues from their Cisco Spark client (Connect feature).

Cisco Hybrid Services empower cloud-based and premises-based solutions to deliver a more capable, better integrated collaboration user experience.

To use Hybrid Services, register your Cisco Expressway with the Collaboration Cloud to install your Connectors. Then, you can monitor and manage Connectors from the Cloud Collaboration Management Portal or from your Cisco Expressways.
